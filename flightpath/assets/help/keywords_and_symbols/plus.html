Symbol: +

The plus sign is used in scan instructions. A scan instruction is in the first brackets of a csvpath. It points to lines that should be included in runs.

+ adds lines to the scan. E.g. 3+5 means scan the 4th line and the fifth line. (Remember that line numbers are 0-based).

Usage

This scan instruction picks out the 3rd and 5th lines $[3+5][ print("hello") ]
This scan instruction includes the first 50 lines and all lines after the 100th. $[0-49+99*][ print("hello") ]
