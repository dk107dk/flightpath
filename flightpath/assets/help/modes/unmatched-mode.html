Mode: unmatched-mode

unmatched-mode configures CsvPath Framework to keep or drop unmatched lines. The values are keep or no-keep. Unmatched mode is only used with named-paths group runs.

With unmatched-mode:keep all unmatched lines land in unmatched.csv. With no-keep the unmatched lines are discarded.

Usage

This csvpath keeps its unmatched lines. They will be found in unmatched.csv in the run directory, along with data.csv and the other typical run artifacts.
