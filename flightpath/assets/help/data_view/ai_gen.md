## Generate CsvPath Language With AI

FlightPath integrates with your AI models to for rapid schema and rules generation. You provide a sample, optionally along with any instructions, and the AI will craft the CsvPath Language to validate and upgrade your data.

AI generation works with the most popular AI assistants. It is tested on Claude, Gemini, and ChatGPT. FlightPath also works with local Ollama servers and many open models; however, results are of a lower quality and require significant compute resources.

The csvpath statements you generate are working first steps. Your AI assistant will validate that the statement(s) work and, as far as the AI knows, are what you need. Pragmatically speaking, you should treat the generated CsvPath Language as a starting point. You will be able to fine-tune or extend your statements to meet needs that an AI cannot be expected to suss out from a brief description.

After you generate the initial csvpath statements, you can use the `Ask a question` dialog, available from the context menu when you have a csvpath file open.
