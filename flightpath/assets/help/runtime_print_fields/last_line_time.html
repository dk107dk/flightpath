Print field: last_line_time

last_line_time is the wall-clock duration of the evaluation of the most recent line.

Generally, this will be a very tiny number. Csvpaths are as time-expensive as the sum of the functions they are made of. That means they can vary quite a bit in complex use-cases.

Usage

Use this field like this. $[0][ print("$.csvpath.last_line_time") ]
